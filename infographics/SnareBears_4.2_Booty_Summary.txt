  • 4.2 was almost entirely a Stanton patch — all top 4 hunting
    grounds were in the Stanton system. Crusader and its moons
    (Cellin, Yela) plus CRU-L1 accounted for 88.9% of all raids.

  • RMC farming was the dominant target economy: Recycled Material
    Composite made up 63.3% of all stolen commodity instances —
    the highest concentration of any single commodity across all
    patches tracked.

  • Reclaimer + Vulture together were targeted in 61.5% of all
    raids with a known ship — RMC salvagers were firmly in the
    crosshairs.

  • Ma6num narrowly edged wardawg in total value (34.9M vs 32.3M)
    despite wardawg running 2 more raids.

  • 4.2's biggest single score (3.5M) was the lowest across all
    patches tracked — consistent with the lower-value RMC-focused
    target pool.
